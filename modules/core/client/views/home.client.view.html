More About ISAC.

ISAC Will represent the international students enrolled at GSU and provide a forum to voice the needs and concerns of the international students to International Services and to the Georgia State University administration. Will provide culturally sensitive programming, which recognizes, supports, and celebrates the diversity of the students and their perspectives. Will plan and sponsor events that unite the international students and educate all students, the GSU community and the Atlanta area regarding the importance and existence of cultural diversity. Will offer leadership training for members of each association under the umbrella of ISAC
